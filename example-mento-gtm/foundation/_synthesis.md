# What we currently believe wins deals at Mento

> Rolling doc. Updated whenever a new pattern is confirmed by ≥2 won deals or a senior-rep+founder agreement. Last 3 entries always at the top.

## Current top beliefs

**1. CHRO/VP-People hire 45–120 days pre-close is the single highest-signal trigger.** Of the last 12 wins audited, 9 had a new people-leader landing in the 45–120 day window before the first Mento conversation. None of those 9 came inbound via marketing — they came warm-introd or outbound-touched after the hire was public on LinkedIn. `[VERIFIED: avoma:11-discovery-calls-2025-Q4 + crunchbase:exec-changes-extract]`

**2. Series B-to-D is the structural sweet spot, not just a funding stage filter.** Pre-Series-B = no people budget. Post-Series-D = either in-house comp team or PE-owned with mandated tooling. Mento wins when there's a comp problem and a buyer who can sign $50–200k without procurement. `[INFERRED: 12/12 wins fall in B–D range, 0/5 pre-B attempts converted]`

**3. Headcount ≥150 is when comp benchmarking becomes a board topic.** Below 150, founders eyeball it. Above 150, the next compensation review has to be defensible. The pain is *defensibility under board scrutiny*, not "we need a spreadsheet." Mento's positioning needs to land that distinction in the first call. `[VERIFIED: avoma:7-of-9-wins-mention-board-defensibility-in-discovery]`

## Open questions (next win-audit cycle)

- **Is "Series E+ with no in-house comp team" a hidden segment?** Two wins fit it but the sample is too small. Re-check in 60 days.
- **Does industry matter inside B2B SaaS?** Current wins skew toward vertical SaaS (4) and dev tools (3). Horizontal SaaS only 2 wins. Could be timing, could be fit. Need a 2026-Q3 audit.
- **What's the actual decision-maker map?** CHRO is the hire-trigger, but who signs? Pattern is unclear — sometimes CHRO, sometimes CFO, sometimes founder. Need to map across the last 12 wins.

## Beliefs being demoted

- ~~"Inbound demos convert better than outbound."~~ → Wrong. The lake showed 9/12 wins had a public trigger 45–120 days pre-close that we missed. Inbound was just the discoverable path. (Demoted 2026-05-09.)
